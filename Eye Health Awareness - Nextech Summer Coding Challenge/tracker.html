Is your screen time necessary (i.e. for work)?

Well, if it is necessary, please follow these important tips that can help reduce eye strain:

  1. Make sure to follow the 20-20-20 rule - see something 20 feet away for 20 seconds every 20 minutes.
  2. Maintain good posture. The following bullet points and the illustration explain what proper "posture" and ergonomics at the desk are:
    • Keep Moniter an arm's length away.
    • Sit with a straight back.
    • Bend your knees at 90 degrees and put both feet flat on the ground.
    • Have 2-3 inches of space between thigh and chair.
    • Keep wrists straight.
    • Keep hands at elbow level.
    • Don't sit for too long at a time. Take breaks!
