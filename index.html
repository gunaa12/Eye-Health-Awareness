Eye Health: Covid-19

Along with you nose and mouth, your eyes are another frequent spot the infection uses to get through to inside our body. It has also been noted that COVID-19 can temporarily hurt visibility or completely blind someone. Sometimes, eyes can be used to detect whether a person has COVID-19 (pink eyes are associated with the infection). Although not much is known, it is possible that even tears can spread the virus.
